I guess we're doing this. We are happy to annouce DC_618, the metro east Defcon group.

November 23, 2016 / by Alex Parker

Hello everyone in the infosec community. We are pleased to announce the creation of a new Defcon group. Our group will be meeting every last Wednesday of the month at 6:30 PM at the Midwest Cyber Center. Check them out at https://midwestcybercenter.org/

Each meeting we will have a guest speaker talk about something related to the infosec community. These talks can be about new tools, interesting research, lessons learned, new threats to watch for, and much more.

So please come out and join us for an enjoyable evening of networking and learning.
